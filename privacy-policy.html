Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 15, 2023

Your Data Stays With You

This application runs entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information.

At LocalFit, we believe your privacy is a fundamental right. Unlike many applications today, we've designed our fitness tool to respect your privacy by keeping all your data locally on your device.

No Data Collection Policy

We want to be perfectly clear: We do not collect any personal information from you. This includes:

  • No account registration required
  • No email addresses collected
  • No fitness data sent to servers
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No location data gathered
  • No payment information (the app is free)

How is this possible? Our application is built as a client-side web app that runs completely in your browser. All processing happens on your device, and no data ever leaves your computer or phone.

How Our Application Works

Our fitness application operates differently from most web applications:

Client-Side Only

All code executes in your browser using JavaScript. No server-side processing required.

Local Data Storage

Your fitness plans and progress are stored locally on your device using browser storage.

No External Requests

The app doesn't make requests to external servers to fetch or send your data.

Instant Loading

Since everything is local, the app loads quickly and works even without internet.

About Local Storage

To provide a personalized experience, our application may use your browser's local storage to save:

  • Your fitness goals and preferences
  • Workout routines you've created
  • Progress tracking data
  • Application settings

Important: This data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party. You have complete control over this data and can clear it at any time through your browser settings.

No Tracking Cookies

We do not use any tracking cookies, analytics services, or advertising networks. The application may use technically necessary cookies to remember your preferences, but these are stored locally and cannot be used to track you across websites.

Since we don't use cookies for tracking, you won't see a cookie consent banner on our site. We respect your privacy by default.

No Third-Party Services

Many applications rely on third-party services for analytics, hosting, or functionality. We don't. Our application is completely self-contained:

  • No Google Analytics or other tracking
  • No social media integrations
  • No advertising networks
  • No external CDNs for libraries (everything is self-hosted)

Please note: While our application itself doesn't collect data, your browser and internet service provider may still collect information about your general usage patterns as part of normal internet operation. This is outside of our control.

Your Data, Your Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is accessible through the application interface
  • Modification: You can edit or delete any information at any time
  • Export: You can export your data if you wish to back it up or move it elsewhere
  • Deletion: Clear your browser data to completely remove all application information

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our approach to data collection (which we don't anticipate), we will:

  1. Clearly announce the changes before they take effect
  2. Explain exactly what data would be collected and why
  3. Provide an option to continue using the application without data collection
  4. Never retroactively apply new data collection to existing users without explicit consent

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • By email: privacy@localfit.app
  • Through our website: localfit.app/contact

We're committed to maintaining your privacy and welcome any questions about how our application works.
